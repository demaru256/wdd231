Uganda Agricultural Explorer

Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

Uganda Agricultural Explorer

The name was chosen because the website allows users to explore agricultural information in Uganda. Visitors will learn about major crops, farming regions, and agricultural practices. The word Explorer represents discovering and learning about Uganda’s agricultural resources.

Optional Domain: ugandaagricultureexplorer.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Uganda Agricultural Explorer website is to provide structured information about agriculture in Uganda. The site will allow visitors to explore important crops, farming regions, and agricultural practices.

Users will be able to view crop information, filter crops by type, save favorite crops, and submit farming tips or questions using a contact form.

The website will include clear navigation to help users easily move between pages and find information quickly.

Scenarios

  • What are the major food crops grown in Uganda?
  • Which crops are mainly produced as cash crops in Uganda?
  • Which region in Uganda grows crops like coffee or tea?
  • How can I submit farming advice or ask agricultural questions?

Color Scheme

The website will use natural colors that represent agriculture.

Dark Green (#2E7D32) – used for headers, navigation bar, and footer.

Light Green (#A5D6A7) – used for page background and accents.

Earth Brown (#6D4C41) – used for buttons and highlights.

White (#FFFFFF) – used for content background and readability.

Typography

Poppins – used for all headings (<h1>, <h2>)

Open Sans – used for paragraphs (<p>) and lists (<li>)
